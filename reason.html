Question 4
What is a characteristic of spear phishing?
Ans: c) It targets a specific individual or organization
Reason:
Spear phishing is characterised by its use of personalised and targeted messaging to target particular people or groups. By taking advantage of the familiarity and trust between the sender and the recipient, this strategy makes the attack more effective and makes it more difficult to identify and defend against.

Ans: Altering the sender's display name to mimic a legitimate domain
Reason:

Question 15
Why is inspecting the email header useful in identifying phishing?
Ans: c) It provides insight into the email's origin and authenticity
Reason:
Why is inspecting the email header useful in identifying phishing? A mismatch between the stated sender in the email body and the actual source in the header can reveal deceptive tactics used by phishers.
