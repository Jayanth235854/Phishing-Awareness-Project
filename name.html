Quiz Guide

Total number of questions is 20. Once you choose the option of your opinion automatically you will be able to find whether your answer is correct or wrong answer. Once your answer is correct it will display in green color and once your option is wrong it will display in red color. Then you can find the scoring board on top right corner, were you can only view score for your correct answers. If you want you can attend the quiz again, by clicking try again button. If you want to go back to the question the click the previous button. There will be a reason button where you can gain knowledge about questions with correct answer. After completing the level 1 click the button of level - 2.
