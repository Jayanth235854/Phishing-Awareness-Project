PHISH-GUARD


A Phishing Awareness Game

PhishGuard is an interactive quiz game designed to educate and raise awareness about phishing techniques. It uses a user-centric approach, combining gamification elements with real-life scenarios. Players encounter simulated phishing attempts, learning to differentiate between genuine and malicious communications, recognize red flags, and develop a critical eye for potential cyber threats. The game includes progress tracking, rewards, and analytics tools for continuous improvement. PhishGuard aims to equip users of all ages and technical backgrounds with essential skills to safeguard themselves against phishing attacks in today's digital landscape.
